We review fees and coverage upfront so you know what to expect before scheduling.

- Cigna and Evernorth
- Optum
- Oscar Health
- Oxford

## Self-pay options
- Initial evaluation (confirm rate during scheduling)
- Follow-up medication management (confirm rate during scheduling)
- Consultation or second opinion (confirm rate during scheduling)

## Payment methods
- ACH bank transfer
- Check
- PayPal
- Venmo

## Financial notes
- Fees may vary by visit type and complexity; everything is confirmed prior to scheduling.
- Sliding scale may be available; ask to see if you are eligible.
- Receipts and superbills are available upon request for out-of-network reimbursement and HSA/FSA documentation.
- Payment and insurance are verified before booking to avoid surprises.
