Reach out to request an appointment, verify insurance, or ask general practice questions.

## How to contact
- Submit the contact form on the website to request telehealth or in-person visits.
- Call the practice at +1-978-648-3935 for quick scheduling questions.
- Email communication may be available after intake; avoid sharing sensitive details over unsecured channels.

## Response and scheduling
- Typical response is within one business day.
- Telehealth instructions are provided after scheduling is confirmed.
- Licensed in Massachusetts and New Hampshire; visits are offered across both states, with in-person appointments available at 1097 Central Street, Leominster, MA.

## Emergencies
The contact form and phone line are not emergency services. If you are in crisis or concerned about immediate safety, call 911, go to the nearest emergency room, or contact the Suicide & Crisis Lifeline at 988.
