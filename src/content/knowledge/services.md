We provide outpatient psychiatric care for adults in Massachusetts and New Hampshire with telehealth and in-person options. Visits are paced, collaborative, and focused on clear next steps.

## Service types
- **Initial psychiatric evaluation:** A thorough intake to understand your history, goals, current concerns, and past treatments. Includes a collaborative plan and recommendations if care is a good fit.
- **Medication management follow-up:** Focused visits to review response, side effects, and adjust medications when appropriate. Includes a clear follow-up schedule.
- **Consultation / second opinion:** Concise review of your current treatment with practical recommendations you can discuss with your care team.

## What to expect
- Trauma-informed, respectful approach; we go at a pace that feels comfortable.
- Clear communication about next steps, visit length, and follow-up cadence.
- Coordination with your therapist or primary care clinician when helpful and permitted.
- Fees and insurance are reviewed and verified before scheduling.

## Locations and licensure
- Telehealth is available across MA and NH when clinically appropriate and permitted by licensure.
- In-person visits are offered by appointment at 1097 Central Street, Leominster, MA.
- Some situations may require in-person follow-up; we will advise during scheduling.

## Emergencies
This practice does not provide emergency or inpatient care. If you are in crisis or concerned about safety, call 911, go to the nearest emergency room, or contact the Suicide & Crisis Lifeline at 988.
