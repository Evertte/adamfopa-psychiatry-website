We keep onboarding simple for telehealth or in-person visits so you know what happens next.

## Steps to get started
1) **Request an appointment:** Choose telehealth or in-person and share your availability and location.
2) **Complete the intake:** A brief form to understand your goals, history, and current medications.
3) **First visit:** Thoughtful evaluation, recommendations, and a clear follow-up plan if care is a good fit.

## Forms
- Intake form (PDF) and consent forms are shared securely once scheduling is confirmed.
- HIPAA notice and other documents are available upon request.

## Telehealth checklist
- Quiet, private space with reliable internet.
- Test camera and audio before the visit.
- Have your ID and current medication list ready.
- Join a few minutes early to check your connection.

## Policies to know early
- Outpatient practice only; emergencies are referred to 911 or the nearest ER.
- Please provide at least 24 hours' notice for cancellations.
- Payment method and insurance eligibility are reviewed before scheduling.
