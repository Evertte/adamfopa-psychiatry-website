These policies keep care predictable and safe for everyone.

## Scope and safety
- Outpatient practice only; we do not provide emergency or inpatient services.
- For emergencies or safety concerns, call 911, go to the nearest emergency room, or contact the Suicide & Crisis Lifeline at 988.

## Scheduling and cancellations
- Request appointments through the contact form or by phone.
- Please provide at least 24 hours notice for cancellations when possible.
- Telehealth availability depends on your location (MA or NH) and clinical appropriateness.

## Communication
- The website form is for scheduling and general questions; do not include sensitive medical details.
- Coordination with your therapist or primary care clinician can be arranged with your permission.

## Payment and insurance
- Fees and insurance are verified prior to scheduling.
- Receipts and superbills are available upon request.
